# SPRINT_ROADMAP

## Recommended Sequence

## Sprint 3
- Authentication
- Audit foundation
- RBAC matrix baseline

Justification: secure foundation required before all multi-user domains.

## Sprint 4
- Organizations
- Users and Teams

Justification: tenant and identity structure required for all business data.

## Sprint 5
- Workspaces and Projects
- Administration settings baseline

Justification: workspace boundaries and policy controls before campaign operations.

## Sprint 6
- Integrations Foundation (connector framework, OAuth, token lifecycle, sync orchestration)

Justification: enables downstream campaign and analytics ingestion.

## Sprint 7
- Google Ads connector
- Meta Ads connector
- GA4 connector

Justification: highest business-value channels first.

## Sprint 8
- Campaign domain implementation
- Analytics canonical model + ingestion

Justification: core workflow and data model now unlocked by connectors.

## Sprint 9
- Reports and Dashboards
- Export/scheduling baseline

Justification: converts ingested data into decision-ready outputs.

## Sprint 10
- AI Assistant phase 1 (prompt/context/streaming)

Justification: AI depends on stable analytics and reporting context.

## Sprint 11
- Notifications service
- Alerting and delivery preferences

Justification: required for operational UX and async workflows.

## Sprint 12
- Billing and entitlements baseline

Justification: monetization after core value path is established.

## Sprint 13
- AI Agents phase 1
- Tool orchestration and job execution controls

Justification: advanced automation after assistant and core data services are mature.

## Sprint 14
- Production hardening
- Performance, security, operational readiness closure

Justification: final stabilization before broad scale rollout.

## Sprint Cadence Rules
- Each sprint reserves 20% capacity for quality/security/observability debt burn-down.
- No sprint starts without signed-off API/event/data contract backlog for targeted domains.
